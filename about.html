About Us

Do you remember that feeling when a song hits you deep in your soul, and it takes you to some other era? That same feeling gave us the idea to create Remio. It's a beautiful blend of those moments when you get lost in memories and the magic that only music can create.

We named it Remio, combining two things we love: those warm feelings of nostalgia and the reassuring company that radio has always been in our lives. Have you ever had that feeling driving alone when your favorite nostalgic song comes on the radio? Your heart skips a beat, and you're transported back to that moment—maybe it was a summer night when you first held hands with a loved one, or a road trip with your best friends, singing at the top of your lungs.

And within the name, there is a little secret, too—it resonates a little like Romeo (yes, that very Romeo!). For much like that eternal love story, music does have this amazing power to speak deep into our souls and bring people together. No matter where you come from or what language you speak, when just the right song hits the air, we all find its echo in our hearts.

But Remio is more than just a place for music. Imagine it as that cozy corner in your favorite café, where every song feels like it was selected specifically for you. It's the place where your first dance at the wedding is forever immortalized, where your mother's favorite lullaby still echoes, and where the song of your graduation still brings a tear to your eye.

We built Remio with lots of love because we really believe in the magic of those goosebump moments when a song is just perfectly in sync with the feelings of your heart. For all those times when you feel lonely, when you want to celebrate, or just when you want to close your eyes and let the music hug you.

This is your sanctuary now, where every song tells your story and every melody is full of the warmth of homecoming. Welcome to Remio, my friend. Let's weave some beautiful memories together.
